# 💼 Business Problem

## 📌 Problem Statement

Modern organizations collect customer information from multiple business systems.

As a result, customer data becomes fragmented across different platforms.

Examples include:

- 👤 CRM
- 🛒 Transaction Systems
- 🎫 Customer Support
- 📦 Product Catalog
- 📢 Marketing Campaigns

Since these systems operate independently, business teams cannot obtain a complete customer profile.

---

# ⚠️ Challenges

- Customer information is scattered across multiple systems.
- Manual reporting consumes significant time.
- Customer insights are incomplete.
- Business teams lack a unified customer view.
- Decision-making becomes slower and less accurate.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

Build a centralized **Customer360 Analytics Platform** using Azure Data Engineering services.

The solution integrates all business datasets into a unified Gold Layer through the Medallion Architecture.

The ETL pipeline performs:

- 📥 Data Ingestion
- 🧹 Data Cleaning
- 🔄 Data Transformation
- 🔗 Data Integration
- 🏆 Customer360 Gold Layer Generation

---

# 🚀 Business Benefits

- 📊 Complete customer visibility
- ⚡ Automated ETL pipeline
- 📈 Better reporting capabilities
- 🎯 Improved customer insights
- ☁️ Scalable cloud architecture
- 🏢 Enterprise-ready analytics platform
